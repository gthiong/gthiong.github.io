This project is a restaurant website. I built it to showcase my skills in developing web app in Bootstrap. The website is responsive and has all pages that a restaurant would need for business growth.

The inbuilt booking page of the website help in regulating the number of customers that the restaurant can hold at one given time. This can help in reducing churn when turning away customers but more importantly, maintaing social-distancing

Online preence is an essential proponent of any business in the age of IoT. Even moreso in the age of covid-19. Having an appealing website increases sales and growth and if you doubt then read Why Restaurants Need a Website.

Interested individuals or restaurant owners who want a something similar to this web app or even better can contact me.
